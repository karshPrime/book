# Vectors

- allow the collective use of groups of signals called vectors
- similar to arrays as they may be subscripted
```vhdl
C(3) <= B(3) or A(3);
C(2) <= B(2) or A(2);
C(1) <= B(1) or A(1);
C(0) <= B(0) or A(0);
```
